Trail Overview:

  • White: Trail running along main fireroad tracks with no tricky singletrack. Great for a day out walking.
  • Blue: Slightly shorter than the white. More family friendly. Once again no singletrack. For riders with more endurance.
  • Singletrack: Natural singletrack trails. Good for more experienced riders. Speak at the shop for advice on where to find these. Unsuitable for walking.
